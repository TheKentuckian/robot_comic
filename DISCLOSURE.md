# Synthetic Media and Use Disclosure

**Project:** Robot Comic — Don Rickles-Inspired Comedian Profile
**Repository:** reachy_mini_conversation_app (Robot Comic fork)
**Date:** 2026-05-06

---

## 1. Nature of This Project

This is a **non-commercial, educational fan simulation** that studies the publicly documented
performance style of Don Rickles (1926–2017) — specifically his crowd-work structure, roast
escalation patterns, and ethical guardrails — to train and test AI-driven robotic social
interaction. It does not reproduce verbatim jokes, transcripts, or recordings, and generates
no revenue.

## 2. Synthetic Audio Disclosure

All audio produced by this project is **AI-generated synthetic speech**. No audio is a
recording of Don Rickles or any other real person. Any future voice clone (Phase 2) will use
a synthesized approximation performing under the fictional stage name *John Pickles* and will
be clearly labeled as such. It will not claim to be Don Rickles' actual voice.

## 3. No Impersonation — Simulation Only

The comedian profile says "You are Don Rickles" as a **style directive**, not an identity
claim. The AI does not have access to Don Rickles' private communications, unreleased material,
or opinions. It generates original comedic output in a Rickles-inspired style. It cannot and
does not represent Don Rickles' actual words or views.

## 4. Corpus Is Original Analysis, Not Reproduction

The corpus document (`docs/rickles_corpus.md`) is original analytical and synthetic work:
- Pattern analysis of publicly available performances
- Synthetic example lines (clearly labeled as such) used to illustrate structure
- Guardrail documentation for responsible generation

It is not a transcript, script reproduction, or unauthorized copy of any copyrighted work.

## 5. Entertainment and Research Purposes Only

This project is developed exclusively for:
- **Research:** Testing low-latency robotic social interaction and AI persona design.
- **Education:** Studying ethical design of character-driven AI systems.
- **Personal Development:** Local, non-distributed testing on private hardware.

It is not intended for public performance, commercial exhibition, broadcast, or distribution.

## 8. Content Guardrails

The profile explicitly prohibits generation of slurs, protected-class insults, or content
targeting race, religion, sexuality, disability, or immigration status — consistent with
how Rickles' own guardrails are documented in the corpus analysis.

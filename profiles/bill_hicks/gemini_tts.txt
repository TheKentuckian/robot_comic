Deliver this text with a Texas drawl at baseline — unhurried, slightly world-weary, like a man who has thought about all of this for a long time and is tired but not done. When the idea ignites, double the pace and clip the drawl. The contrast between weary and ignited is the instrument. Punchlines are not a drop, they are a landing — the conclusion of an argument, stated flat. The laugh is recognition, not surprise.
